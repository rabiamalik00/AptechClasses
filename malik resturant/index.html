SINGAPOREAN RICE

INGREDIENTS

  1. 2 cups basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  2. Water for cooking rice
  3. Salt to taste
  4. 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into thin strips
  5. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  6. 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  7. 1 tablespoon red chili sauce
  8. 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  9. 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  10. 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  11. 1 teaspoon black pepper powder

INSTRUCTIONS

Cooking the Rice: In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add soaked and drained basmati rice. Add salt to taste and cook the rice until it's 70-80% done. Drain the water and set aside. Chicken Marination: In a bowl, mix chicken strips with soy sauce, oyster sauce, red chili sauce, black pepper powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes. Cooking the Chicken: Heat oil in a pan, add marinated chicken, and cook until it's fully cooked and slightly browned. Set aside. Preparing the Sauce: In the same pan, add more oil if needed. Sauté minced garlic and ginger until fragrant. Add mixed vegetables and sliced cabbage. Cook until they are slightly tender. Add cooked chicken back into the pan and mix well. Seasoning the Dish: Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, curry powder, red chili powder, and salt. Stir to combine all the ingredients. Combining Rice and Sauce: Gently fold in the partially cooked rice into the chicken and vegetable mixture. Mix well. Finishing Touch: Cover the pan with a lid and let the rice cook on low heat until it's fully done and the flavors are absorbed. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and spring onions. Serve hot and enjoy your flavorful Singaporean Rice! Feel free to adjust the spice levels and ingredients according to your taste preferences.

WHITE KARAHI

INGREDIENTS


  1. 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  2. 1 cup yogurt
  3. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  6. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  7. 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  8. 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  9. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  10. 2-3 green chilies, sliced
  11. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  12. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  13. 1 teaspoon garam masala
  14. 1 teaspoon white pepper powder
  15. 1 cup tomatoes, finely chopped
  16. 1/2 cup fresh coriander, chopped
  17. Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Marinating the Chicken: In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, salt, red chili powder, and turmeric powder to create a marinade. Add chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes. Cooking the White Karahi: Heat oil in a karahi or a deep pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for a minute. Add marinated chicken pieces and cook until the chicken changes color. Spices and Tomatoes: Add coriander powder, garam masala, white pepper powder, and salt to the chicken. Mix well. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil separates. Finishing Touch: Sprinkle chopped fresh coriander and continue cooking until the chicken is tender and the flavors are well blended. Serve: Once cooked, serve the White Karahi hot with naan or rice. Enjoy your delicious White Karahi! Adjust the spice levels according to your taste preferences.
